    Zsosa's Timepiece Abilities
    Momentary Stasis
    Spend 2 Hope Make a spellcasting role against a creature, the targeted creature must make an Instinct roll. If the Instinct roll is lower than the caster's spellcasting roll, the targeted creature is Immobilized until the creature's spotlight returns or the GM spends a Fear to remove it.
    Temporal Awareness
    Spend 1 Hope, start your spotlight phase immediately. If another player is currently in the spotlight, describe how you work with them to take your turn.
    Flicker In Time
    Spend 2 Hope to immediately clear 1 stress from or give 1 Hope to up to 5 allies.
    Zsosa's Timepiece Blessings
    Temporal Awareness
    You can create a 15-foot-radius sphere of temporal distortion, centered on yourself, that allows you and any friendly creature that enters the space to peer into the past and comb through the events of that space, up to 24 hours ago. This effect lasts 10 minutes, or until dismissed
    Timeless Body
    Once fully empowered, for every 10 years that pass your body only ages 1 year. This effect ends if you no longer control the pocket watch.

    Thunderstrike
    Once per short rest, slam the staff against the ground to create a deafening shock wave emanating from you. All adversaries must make an Instinct roll of 18 or are Vulnerable until you take your next turn or the GM spends 1 Fear to end the effect early.
    Per short rest
    Call Lightning
    Spend 1 Hope. Conjure a beam of lightning in the direction you are facing that reaches Very Far range. Any adversary within this area must succeed an Agility roll of 18 or take 5d6 lightning damage.
    Unending Torrent
    Spend 1 Hope to summon a powerful stream of water directed towards an enemy or enemies of your choosing in a line. Adversaries must make a Strength roll of 16 or be pushed into the next furthest range from you. This effect can also be used to affect the environment around you, including putting out non-magical fires.

    Crackling Infusion: Spend 1 Hope to add 2d6 to your next damage roll.

    Untouched by Flame
    Spend 2 Hope, become immune to fire damage until the end of the scene. Removing the diadem also removes this effect.
    Dazzling Light
    Spend 1 Hope, create a sphere of light that reaches to Far range until the end of scene. While within this dome of light, you are unaffected by magical darkness.
    Starfall
    Conjure spectral daggers made of starlight that circle your head. Spend 1 Hope to deal 6d10+8 damage to a creature within Far range.
    Forceful Hold
    Spend 2 Hope, create a force field around a creature that you can see until your next spotlight, or until the GM spends 1 Fear to dispel the effect.
    Dragon Scale Armor
    Predator to Pray
